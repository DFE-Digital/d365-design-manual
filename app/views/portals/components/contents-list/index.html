When to not use this component

Do not use a contents list on pages which aren't grouped together or related as this is likely confuse users.

Do not use a contents list in a transactional service or a form. Use other components instead, such as buttons to move forward and back links to navigate back.

How it works

Keep links short and descriptive. Links that are too long (more than 2 lines) make it difficult for users to scan the list.

Page titles or header titles must reflect the subject of the page or the header text on the page. This is so users are clear where they have navigated to.
